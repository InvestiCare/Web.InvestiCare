How to use Investment Calculator??

  1. Enter the Starting Amount
    • Input the initial amount you are planning to invest.
    • Example: ₹10,000
  2. Set the Annual Return Rate (%)
    • Enter the expected annual return percentage from your investment.
    • Example: If your investment is expected to grow at 8% annually, enter 8.
  3. Specify the Investment Return Time (Years)
    • Enter the total number of years you plan to keep your investment.
    • Example: If you are investing for 5 years, enter 5.
  4. Additional Contribution (Optional)
    • If you plan to add money regularly to your investment, enter the additional amount you will contribute each time.
    • Example: ₹1,000 added per month.
  5. Select the Contribution Interval
    • Choose how often you will add additional contributions:
    • Monthly (Every month)
    • Quarterly (Every 3 months)
    • Annually (Every year)
  6. Choose Contribution Added Type
    • Decide whether your additional contribution is:
    • Beginning of the period – Contributions are added at the start of the selected interval.
    • End of the period – Contributions are added at the end of the selected interval.
  7. Click 'Calculate'
    • Press the Calculate button to get the estimated future value of your investment.
